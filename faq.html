FAQ

Common questions, answered

Everything you need to know before starting a project with SPO Digital Verse.

GENERAL
What makes SPO Digital Verse builds different?
We build offline-first architectures, so your systems stay usable even without a stable internet connection — something most off-the-shelf software doesn't handle well.
Do you work with international clients?
Yes. We deliver automation, ERP/POS and web projects for clients across Pakistan and internationally, fully remote from discovery to launch.
What industries do you build for?
Retail, restaurants, real estate, clinics, salons, printing shops, and service-based agencies — our systems are reconfigured for each business type.
Is SPO Digital Verse a registered business?
SPO Digital Verse operates under Muhammad Asif Ali, delivering agency-style services with direct founder involvement on every project.
AI AUTOMATION
What kind of automation can you build?
WhatsApp/chatbot automation, AI-powered customer replies, lead capture and follow-up workflows, and internal tools that reduce manual repetitive tasks.
Can the AI automation handle multiple languages?
Yes, our automation systems are built to reply in both Urdu and English depending on how your customers write to you.
Does the AI automation connect to WhatsApp directly?
Yes, we integrate with WhatsApp Business for automated replies, order updates, and lead capture flows.
POS & ERP SYSTEMS
Can you build a custom POS/ERP for my industry?
Yes — our POS/ERP systems are built to be reconfigured across retail, services, and multi-branch business models.
What happens if my internet goes down?
Our systems are built offline-first with local data storage (IndexedDB), automatically syncing to the cloud once connectivity returns.
Can multiple branches use the same system?
Yes, our ERP/POS platforms support multi-branch inventory, billing, and ledger management synced through a central cloud database.
Does the system support Udhaar/Khata (credit ledger) tracking?
Yes, customer credit balances, partial payments, and ledger history are all supported as a core feature.
Can staff have different access levels?
Yes, role-based access control lets you assign different permissions to owners, managers, and staff members.
WEBSITE DEVELOPMENT
Do you build e-commerce websites?
Yes, we build e-commerce and booking-enabled websites along with standard business and portfolio sites.
Will my website work well on mobile devices?
Yes, every website we build is fully responsive and tested across mobile, tablet, and desktop screens.
Can you redesign my existing website?
Yes, we can redesign and modernize existing websites while preserving your content, SEO rankings, and branding.
SEO & MARKETING
How long does SEO take to show results?
Typically 2-4 months for noticeable ranking improvements, depending on competition and your website's starting point.
Do you manage social media accounts directly?
Yes, our digital marketing service includes content creation, posting schedules, and campaign management for your social platforms.
Can you help with Google Business Profile ranking?
Yes, local SEO including Google Business Profile optimization is part of our SEO service offering.
PRICING & PAYMENTS
How is pricing determined?
Every project is scoped individually based on features, complexity, and timeline. We provide a custom quote after understanding your requirements.
What payment methods do you accept?
Bank transfer, JazzCash, EasyPaisa, and international payment options depending on project location.
Do you require an advance payment?
Yes, projects typically begin with an agreed advance, followed by milestone-based payments depending on project size.
Are there any hidden costs after project delivery?
No, all costs are agreed upfront in the project quote. Any additional feature requests outside the original scope are quoted separately before work begins.
SUPPORT & GETTING STARTED
Do you provide ongoing support after launch?
Yes, all packages include a support period post-launch, with optional extended support and feature development afterward.
How long does a typical project take?
Timelines depend on scope — simple websites can take 1-2 weeks, while full ERP/POS or automation systems typically take 4-8 weeks.
Will I be trained on how to use the system?
Yes, we provide hands-on training for your team as part of the launch process, along with documentation where needed.
How do I get started?
Reach out via our contact form, WhatsApp, or email with a brief description of your business and needs — we'll schedule a discovery call.

Still have questions?

Reach out directly and we'll get back to you within 24 hours.
